Welcome to Apocalypse Fiction

Like your literature with a darker twist? This site brings together people who enjoy fiction in the setting of an apocalypse, for book reviews and recommendations.

Stories that enthrall

Although it may sound depressing as a theme, apocalyptic literature gives a rich setting for exploring societal values, how interpersonal dynamics change under stress, and the essence of what it is to be human.

Characters are all stretched to their limits by the uncertain world. Readers are forced to re-examine their relationship with themselves and with others. Writers get to explore fear and courage, self-interest and self-sacrifice, in a strange new world.

All from the comfort of one's own sofa.
